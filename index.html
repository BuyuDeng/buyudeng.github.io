My name is Buyu Deng(邓卜瑜). I am currently a second-year undergraduate student at the School of the Gifted Young, University of Science and Technology of China, majoring in Geophysics, with a minor in Computer Science. Under the mentorship of Dr. Xinming Wu, I am actively conducting research on the application of deep learning techniques to geophysical problems.

Research Interests

Deep Learning for Geophysical Problems

My research interests lie at the intersection of deep learning and geophysics, with a focus on applying machine learning techniques to address complex challenges in geophysical data analysis.
